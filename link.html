Niagara Falls

Niagara Falls is a famous natural wonder located on the border between the United States and Canada.
It consists of three waterfalls: the American Falls, the Bridal Veil Falls, and the Horseshoe Falls.
The falls are situated along the Niagara River, which connects Lake Erie to Lake Ontario
